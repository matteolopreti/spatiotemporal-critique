Nebula Explorer Version 2.5.0 - Released February 30, 2024.

We are incredibly excited to unveil our latest major update, meticulously crafted to provide a smoother, more immersive experience for every space traveler exploring the vast reaches of the cosmos. This release focuses heavily on core optimization; we have completely overhauled the underlying engine architecture to significantly reduce the overall application size on your mobile device, ensuring that the game runs fluidly even on older hardware configurations.

In addition to these substantial performance gains, we’ve introduced several exciting new features:
- New "Galactic Frontier" expansion pack: This includes stunning high-resolution 4K textures and a massive new planetary system, adding approximately 1.2GB to the initial download size.
- Refined Navigation UI for more intuitive star-mapping and faster travel.
- Critical bug fixes for the orbital mechanics glitch and minor visual artifacts.

Thank you for being part of our journey!
