- Assumption: “all workspace tiers” includes every hosted paid/free tier, but not necessarily self-hosted. If wrong, availability claims may mislead customers.

**5. Three Angles Merged**

Domain expert: Strong concern around absolute security and performance language. Search indexing body text plus permission filtering is a common source of leakage through snippets, counts, cached indexes, or stale ACLs.

Skeptical generalist: The text is credible, but “all tiers,” “under 300 ms,” “only ever,” and “requires no migration” read like claims that need scope.

End user: The release sounds useful and understandable. The likely confusion points are whether their workspace qualifies, whether search will really be that fast for them, and what happens to comments if they close the browser while offline.

Contested: “Under 300 ms at p95” may be a valuable precision signal or an over-specific claim depending on whether the notes link to benchmark conditions. Without that context, I would not remove it, but I would qualify it.
